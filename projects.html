Crime Stats

Project in development using Google Maps API and UK Police API to create a mobile application that allows visual representation of crimes in selected areas or streets with details about every crime such as type, date and status. The application will also show statistics, areas covered by police stations and their contact information and clear heatmap representation of crime levels.

Vitautas Kalimba

Project done in partnership with another developer for a small handmade kalimba musical boxes business. It involves developing a one page store for displaying and purchasing different kalimbas, offering details about how they are made and connections to social media using a sleek, modern design.

Explore the Solar System

WebGL, three.js and Leap Motion 3D model of the Solar System. Explore our Solar System by selecting every planet and moving around it using either the mouse or your hands in the air using a Leap Motion bar. The project is to be further developed and include educational facts about every selected planet.
